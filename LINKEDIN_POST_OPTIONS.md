## Tips for Your Post:

1. **Add your portfolio link** - Place it in the post itself or as the first comment
2. **Include relevant hashtags** (3-5 recommended):
   - #SoftwareEngineering #FullStackDeveloper #Portfolio
   - #React #NodeJS #WebDevelopment
   - #TechJobs #DeveloperLife

3. **Post timing**: Best engagement usually comes from posting Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-1 PM in your timezone

4. **Add a visual**: LinkedIn posts with images/videos get 2x more engagement. Consider:
   - A screenshot of your portfolio homepage
   - A GIF showing your portfolio in action
   - A cover image with key technologies/projects

5. **Engage with comments**: Respond to every comment in the first 24 hours for maximum reach
